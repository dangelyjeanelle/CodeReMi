About Us

Online Computer Science Education Made Just for You

Our motivation: Music is a universal language which is often times taught to children from a very young age. This is usually when children are more likely to learn new concepts and ideas. Computer science education, on the other hand, is not as accessible. Most computer science programs require a strong STEM background and even some English knowledge, which means that students who lack this background often feel discouraged from even attempting to learn computer science.

Our goal: To improve the chances of children from diverse backgrounds learning Computer Science and to facilitate learning by presenting it as an application of concepts that students with a music background already know as opposed to starting from scratch.
